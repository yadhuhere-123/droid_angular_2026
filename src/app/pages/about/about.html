Genesis of DROID

DROID 5.0 is the flagship technical symposium organized by IEEE FISAT SB in collaboration with IEEE RAS SBC FISAT. Since its inception, DROID has served as a crucible for innovation — bringing together the brightest minds in robotics and automation under one roof.
